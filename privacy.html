Privacy Policy

Cloud Service EOL Tracker

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Cloud Service EOL Tracker ("we", "our", or "the service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service. This includes:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on pages
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)
  • Referral source

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to:

  • Cache service data for improved performance (expires after 24 hours)
  • Remember your language preference (English or Japanese)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Cookies

We use cookies through Google Analytics to track usage patterns. These cookies:

  • Are used for analytics purposes only
  • Do not store personally identifiable information
  • Can be disabled through your browser settings

For more information about Google Analytics cookies, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Understand how users interact with our service
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Make informed decisions about new features

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties except:

  • Google Analytics, as described above
  • When required by law

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • Regular security updates

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable cookies through your browser settings
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Clear local storage data through your browser settings

Third-Party Links

Our service contains links to external websites (official cloud provider documentation). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date below.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
